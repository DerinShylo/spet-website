Our Origin

The Soosaiya Peter Educational Trust was not founded with walls, but with wisdom. It began as a whisper in the heart — that education is not merely about learning facts, but about nurturing souls.

Our journey started in humble silence, rooted in rural soil, where dreams dared to grow despite the odds. What began with a handful of students and a blackboard is now a constellation of institutions — each echoing our belief: education with a mother's care.

Legacy & Culture

For us, learning is not confined to classrooms. It lives in discipline, empathy, inquiry, and resilience. We believe in holistic growth — of intellect, heart, and community.

Our institutions carry forward this ethos — with modern pedagogy grounded in age-old values. We blend the rigor of science with the rhythm of service.

From the Founder’s Desk

When I laid the first stone of what would one day become the Soosaiya Peter Educational Trust, I wasn’t building a school — I was planting a seed. A seed of belief. A belief that education is the most powerful tool we can offer a child, not just to make a living, but to build a life filled with meaning, dignity, and compassion.

I came from modest beginnings — I know what it is to walk miles for knowledge, to sit under trees and dream of classrooms. I made a promise then: that if I ever found the means, I would ensure no child would ever have to beg for an opportunity to learn.

This trust is not a monument of wealth or prestige. It is a reflection of faith — in children, in teachers, in the slow but certain power of perseverance. We did not begin with grandeur; we began with grit. One building. One teacher. A few benches. And an unwavering spirit.

Today, as our institutions grow and echo with the voices of hundreds of students, my heart remains with that first child who walked through our gates, shy-eyed but full of hope. It is for that child, and all those who followed, that we continue to grow — with care, with conviction, and with the courage to always keep our roots in the soil of service.

To the parents who entrust us, to the faculty who stand with us, and to the students who inspire us — I offer my deepest gratitude. May this trust never forget its purpose: to nurture, to guide, and to lift.

– Dr. Ln.S Peter, Founder, Soosaiya Peter Educational Trust
