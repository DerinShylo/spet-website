Welcome to Our Trust

The Soosaiya Peter Educational Trust is a beacon of hope, learning, and transformation in the field of education. With decades of service, our commitment has always been to provide accessible, value-based, and quality education to every student, regardless of background.

Our journey began with a single institution and a dream — a dream of nurturing young minds into compassionate, skilled, and responsible citizens. Today, we are proud to serve hundreds of students across various schools and colleges under our wing.

Our Pillars

Mission

To provide inclusive, quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and ethical responsibility in young minds.

Vision

To empower generations through education rooted in compassion, integrity, and the pursuit of lifelong learning.

Values

Our core values include honesty, humility, service to humanity, dedication, and love — reflected in every lesson we teach.
